Smart Farm Tool.
Making agriculture easier.





Smart Farm Tool is a free to use tool for farmers. Our vision is to provide farmers with an easy to use tool for managing their expenses. The tool will also provide farmers with profitable selling prices for their produce. The tool has many more features.
